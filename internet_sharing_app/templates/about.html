About NetShare

NetShare is committed to making internet connectivity accessible to everyone, even in remote areas. Our platform allows users to request, pay for, and access encrypted internet configurations shared by hosts globally.

We believe in decentralizing internet access by enabling secure and timed sharing, ensuring everyone can stay connected, regardless of location or resources.
